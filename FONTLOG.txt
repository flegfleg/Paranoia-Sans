FONTLOG for the PARANOIA SANS

Basic Font Information

PARANOIA SANS is a self-censoring, conspiratorial typeface that will automatically redact more than 150 words popular in conspiracy myths/theories.
PARANOIA SANS is a modified version of Courier Prime Sans by  Quote-Unquote Apps.
PARANOIA SANS is a Unicode typeface family with unexpected ligatures. 

To contribute to PARANOIA SANS contact Florian Egermann hallo@fleg.de
